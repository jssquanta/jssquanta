About Us



Quanta,the official technical society of JSSATE,NOIDA aims at innovation and excellence. The organisation remains active around the year through workshops and events dealing in robotics.

It imparts knowledge to students through regular workshops related to mannual and automated robotics .The members are actively involved in organising events and keeping the trend.

Our Earlier Projects

Track-o-Luna

Organised by Quanta at Zealicon, this event had participants building a Line Follower Robot or LFR, which is supposed to follow the path laid down in the shortest time as possible. The students were given time to write the code then and there and make the necessary adjustments.

Shoot 'em up

In the technical and the cultural fest Zealicon,Quanta hosted a number of events,the most popular one being "Shoot 'em up",an event o Mannual Robotics. Each team built a mannual robot in a 3 day workshop by Quanta and competed against each other in a fun yet challenging event.
